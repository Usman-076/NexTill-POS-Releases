# NexTill POS Support

## Contact Support

Contact the NexTill supplier who issued your license for installation, activation, printing, update, or operational assistance.

## Helpful Information

Include:

- What you were trying to do
- What happened
- The exact error message
- Whether the issue happens every time
- NexTill version
- Windows version
- Printer or scanner model when relevant

Do not send customer records, passwords, license keys, payment details, or database backups.

## Installation And Activation

Provide the Installation ID displayed by NexTill when requesting setup or license assistance.

## Security Reports

Follow the private reporting process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Do not publish exploitable security details in a public issue.
