# NexTill POS Support

## In-App Support

Licensed customers should use **Settings → Feedback & Support** inside NexTill.

Choose a category:

- **Bug** for unexpected behavior or errors
- **Suggestion** for product ideas
- **Support** for installation, activation, printing, or operational help
- **Other** for general messages

Feedback is queued locally if the shop is offline and sends automatically when connectivity returns. Replies appear inside the same NexTill support screen.

## Helpful Information

Include:

- What you were trying to do
- What happened
- The exact error message
- Whether the issue happens every time
- NexTill version
- Windows version
- Printer or scanner model when relevant

Do not send customer records, passwords, license keys, payment details, or database backups.

## Diagnostics

Support may request a sanitized diagnostic summary. A shop administrator must approve the request locally before anything is uploaded.

Approved diagnostics can include service health, version details, update history, backup status, sanitized logs, and recent technical errors. They exclude database backups, credentials, license keys, customer data, product details, and sales contents.

Diagnostic bundles are private, limited in size, and automatically expire.

## Installation And Activation

For setup or license assistance, contact the NexTill supplier who issued your license and provide the Installation ID displayed by NexTill.

## Security Reports

Follow the private reporting process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Do not publish exploitable security details in a public issue.
